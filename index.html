Discover. Support. Impact...Together

The desire to be part of a community and to make a difference is shared by people everywhere. Often in our high-tech, fast paced world it is hard to find the time to do what matters most. The Great Hearts Community was created to help with this challenge.

We've just started. Over 300 causes have been recommended so far. Members have a combined social media reach of 350,000. New things are happening daily.
