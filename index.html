Thanks for Considering Great Hearts!

As a founding member, you’ll help create an important new way to support causes, meet friends, and get inspired. Being part of the Great Hearts community is free and will always be free.
In September 2016, the Great Hearts community will launch with a founding group of members. You’ll be part of it. In the meantime, tells us your favorite nonprofit organizations and causes. We’ll reach out to them. Organizations will be eligible for Giver's Choice Awards based on members’ recommendations.
In July and August watch for Griggles — occasional, brief messages to give you a boost and share community updates. You’ll see some of your favorites featured and get your first ribbon tiles for the Giver's Choice Awards.

Thanks! Look out for a verification email from Great Hearts shortly!

GIVING IS GOOD FOR YOUR HEALTH

Health research tells us that giving benefits us both physically and emotionally. Heart disease, diabetes, depression—all are improved as people engage with causes that are meaningful to them. Experience the giving/good health connection by being part of the Great Hearts community.
