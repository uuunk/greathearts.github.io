Thanks for Joining Great Hearts!

By joining the Great Hearts Community now you're helping to create a new way to support causes, meet friends, and get inspired. The Great Hearts Community is free and will always be free.
Some of the things you can do now include:
  • Share your favorite causes.
  • Connect directly to causes recommended by other community members.
  • Welcome organizations as they join the Great Hearts Community.
  • Earn ribbon tiles for the Giver’s Choice Award
  • Share ideas for the design the community’s new web space.
  • Feel good and get the “giver’s glow”.
Watch for Griggles — occasional, brief messages to give you a boost and to share community updates. In October, when the Great Hearts Community will move into its new web space, you’ll be able to create your own profile, connect with people, search over 1.5 million causes and give ribbons tiles for the Giver's Choice Awards.

Share. Inspire. Impact. Together.

The Great Hearts Community helps people live a giving lifestyle. The desire to be part of a community and to make a difference is shared by people everywhere. Often in our high-tech, fast paced world it is hard to find the time to do what matters most. The Great Hearts Community was created to help with this challenge.

We've just started and are still in beta. Over 100 causes have been recommended so far. Members have a combined social media reach of 500,000. New things are happening daily.

Join the Great Hearts Community today and:

Join the Great Hearts Community today and:

  • Share your favorite causes.
  • Welcome organizations as they join the Great Hearts Community.
  • Receive Griggle messages that feature inspiring causes and their programs.
  • Earn ribbon tiles for the Giver’s Choice Award.
  • Help the nonprofits you support have a broader social media reach.
  • Share ideas for the design of the community’s new web space.
  • Feel good and get the “giver’s glow”.

In November, when the new web space opens, you’ll be able to create your own profile, connect with people, search over 1.5 million causes for ones that match your interests, find volunteer opportunities, participate in forums and groups, and help select the nonprofits that will receive the Giver’s Choice Award.
