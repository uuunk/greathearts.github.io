Sponsorship Opportunities

Great Hearts offers sponsorship opportunities for corporations, foundations and individuals. Sponsorship provides a way to meet your firm’s philanthropic and marketing goals. By sponsoring Great Hearts, you are helping to expand the giving community.

Founding Sponsorship

Founding sponsor contributions are used to support our core initiatives. Your contribution enables 100 percent of the individual’s gift to go directly to the cause or nonprofit. This type of sponsorship gives prominent recognition but is not tax deductible.

Sponsorship of the Great Hearts Award

Sponsorship of a Great Hearts Award provides unrestricted contributions to the nonprofit organizations that are selected for the award. As a sponsor, you may either select a specific category of causes (e.g. health, homelessness, conservation) or provide funding for a general (all causes are eligible) award.

Thanks for Joining Great Hearts!

By joining the Great Hearts Community now you're helping to create a new way to support causes, meet friends, and get inspired. The Great Hearts Community is free and will always be free.
Some of the things you can do now include:
  • Share your favorite causes.
  • Connect directly to causes recommended by other community members.
  • Welcome organizations as they join the Great Hearts Community.
  • Earn ribbon tiles for the Great Hearts Award
  • Share ideas for the design the community’s new web space.
  • Feel good and get the “giver’s glow”.
